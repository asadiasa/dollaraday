Despite all the tragedies that we have faced so far in 2020, what amazes me the most is the wilingness of others to help out their community. I started this project to remind ourselves even when the hashtags have stopped trending, organizations still need your help.

The challenge is to save a dollar every week day (M-F). At the end of the week, choose an organization to donate the funds to. As a college student, I know that money can be tight, so if you are unable to donate, I have also listed ways you can start cultivating a habit of giving.
